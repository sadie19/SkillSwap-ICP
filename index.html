Frequently Asked Questions

To post a gig, click on the "Post" link in the navigation bar. Fill out the form with details about your opportunity, including title, description, skills required, and compensation. Once submitted, your gig will be reviewed and published within 24 hours.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway partners.

You can manage your gigs from the "My Gigs" section. Click on the "Edit" button to make changes or "Delete" to remove a gig. Note that once a gig has active applications, certain details cannot be changed.

If you encounter any technical issues or have concerns about another user, please contact our support team immediately through this contact form or via email at support@skillswapicp.com. Include as many details as possible about the issue.
